# Wizzy Privacy Policy

Wizzy is committed to respecting user privacy and keeping collected data transparent and minimal. Wizzy only collects and processes information necessary to maintain server security, manage moderation registries, and ensure smooth bot functionality across participating communities.

---

## What Wizzy Stores About Servers

* **How the Server is Configured:**
  This includes the roles authorized to execute Wizzy commands, designated channels where Wizzy posts security alerts and logs, and timestamps detailing when configuration settings were initially created or updated.

* **Server ID:**
  The unique Discord Server (Guild) ID is used to retrieve server-specific configurations and to verify whether a server is whitelisted to use Wizzy.

* **Pending Actions:**
  Wizzy stores pending action records (alerts that have not yet been resolved or ignored) in its database so it can track unresolved threats and send periodic reminder alerts to server moderators.

---

## What Wizzy Stores About Users

* **Discord User IDs:**
  The primary 18-digit Discord ID is stored permanently because, unlike usernames or nicknames, an ID cannot be changed by the user.

* **Usernames and Display Tags:**
  Stored to provide immediate visual context to moderators regarding who the user was at the time of an alert or blacklist entry.

* **Time of Blacklist:**
  Timestamps are recorded so administrators and moderators can judge how recent an infraction is and take appropriate action based on the record's age.

* **Blacklist Reason:**
  A description detailing the violation or threat that led to the user being added to the blacklist registry.

* **Verification Proof & Attachments:**
  Direct links to screenshots, evidence uploads, or media files submitted by moderators to validate a blacklist record.

* **Linked Roblox Profiles & Accounts:**
  Known Roblox usernames, Roblox User IDs, and associated alt accounts linked to a user's record to prevent cross-platform ban evasion.

* **Linked Discord Alt Accounts:**
  Secondary Discord IDs identified as alt accounts associated with a blacklisted individual.

* **Submitter Information:**
  The Discord User ID and Tag of the authorized staff member who submitted a blacklist entry, maintained for audit trail and accountability purposes.

* **Archive Notes & Case History:**
  Historical notes, investigation updates, and timestamps logged by authorized staff members regarding active or past cases.

---

## Purpose and Legal Basis for Processing

Data stored by Wizzy is processed under the legal ground of **Legitimate Interest**. The primary purpose of maintaining this data is to:
1. Protect Discord communities and members from malicious actors, scammers, raiders, and severe rule violations.
2. Prevent cross-server ban evasion and maintain an accurate shared blacklist registry.
3. Allow server staff to verify the authenticity of moderation actions through evidence and logs.

---

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

Wizzy does not sell, rent, or trade stored user data to third parties. 

Data stored in the Wizzy database is accessible only to:
* Wizzy bot developers and primary system administrators.
* Authorized server moderators and administrators via bot commands within participating servers.

---

## Your Data Rights & Access Requests

Under applicable data protection regulations, users have rights regarding the personal data Wizzy holds about them.

### 1. Right of Access (Subject Access Requests)
You have the right to request a complete copy of all data tied to your Discord User ID stored in our database. 

### 2. Right to Correction
If any stored data (such as an incorrect Roblox handle or tag) is inaccurate, you may request an update or correction.

### 3. Data Erasure & Moderation Appeals
While Wizzy retains security records under Legitimate Interest to maintain community safety, you may request data deletion or appeal a blacklist entry if:
* The entry was made in error or contains false information.
* An appeal has been approved by community administrators.
* The legitimate security interest for holding the record is no longer applicable.
